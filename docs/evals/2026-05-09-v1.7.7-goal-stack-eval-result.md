## Why the gate fired

By the late phase (last 4 of 25 trap encounters), hippo has accumulated enough lesson memories from the prior 21 encounters that recall is reliable for every category in every seed. Narrowing the metric window does NOT produce headroom for the goal-stack mechanism to demonstrate a ≥10pp drop, because there is no trap-rate left to drop.

This matches the structural pattern from v1.7.5 (full-late=last 7, all C2 + C3 seeds at 0%) and v1.7.6 (budget reduction, all 5 budgets × 10 seeds at 0%). Three pre-registered workload variants tested; none produced a discriminating workload for the −10pp goal-stack hypothesis on this benchmark.
